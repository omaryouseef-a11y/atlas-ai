# Atlas Kids Media - Character Bible

## Project: The Great Forest Picnic Journey

1. **Squirrel**: Sokkar (سكر) 🐿️ - Active and bouncy.
2. **Fox**: Felix (فليكس) 🦊 - Smart and fast.
3. **Rabbit**: Bonnie/Bunny (بوني) 🐇 - Gentle and light.
4. **Bear**: Barnaby/Barney (بارني) 🐻 - Kind with a warm voice.
5. **Bird**: Tweety/Berry (تويتي/بيري) 🐦 - Sweet voice.
6. **Deer**: Bambi/Flora (بامبي/فلورا) 🦌 - Loves flowers.
7. **Turtle**: Torti (تورتي) 🐢 - Slow but wise.
8. **Raccoon**: Ricky (ريكي) 🦝 - Playful and clean.
9. **Hedgehog**: Henry (هنري) 🦔 - Small and cute.
10. **Frog**: Freddy (فريدي) 🐸 - Welcoming and friendly.
