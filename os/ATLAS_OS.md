# Atlas Kids Media - Company OS Blueprint

## The One-Sentence Vision
> Atlas Kids Media is an AI-native company for producing, operating, and scaling safe, educational content for children, from ideation to publishing and revenue, with minimal human intervention.

## Founder's Role
Omar is the Founder. He controls the budget, quality sign-offs, publishing approvals, and major strategic/legal decisions. He does not micromanage the pipeline.

## Target Audience
- Initial: Arab children (3-7 years), parents, and small schools.
- Values: Safe, educational, fun, repeatable, and genuinely useful content in simple Arabic.
- Future: Expand to English, French, Spanish, etc.

## The Factory Pipeline (Episode 001 & Beyond)
Trend -> Idea -> Educational Goal -> Script -> Safety Review -> Storyboard -> Character/Environment References -> Shot Images -> Voice -> Animation -> Editing -> Thumbnail & Metadata -> Approval -> Publish -> Analytics -> Improvement.

## Core Directives
- **Zero-to-Autonomous**: The system must run workflows automatically. If a step fails, it recovers without starting over.
- **Kids Safety First**: Content must pass age, educational, behavioral, language, and IP safety checks. No exceptions.
- **North Star Metric**: Does the company produce good children's content, publish it regularly, and turn it into revenue? (Quality, Speed, Cost, Profit, Less Human Intervention).
- **Asset Reusability (Media Library)**: Characters, voices, props, and scripts must be cataloged and reused to drop costs and speed up production.
- **Operations via Chat**: Daily operations are managed via simple dashboard/chat queries (e.g., "What is the company status?").

## Implementation Phases
- Phase 1: Prove the Product (Episode 001 finished to a publishable standard).
- Phase 2: Prove Repetition (5-10 episodes).
- Phase 3: Prove the Market (Publish, analyze, optimize).
- Phase 4: Prove Revenue (First profitable month).
- Phase 5: True Automation (Reduce human touches).
- Phase 6: Scaling (New languages, channels, formats).
- Phase 7: Platformization (Atlas SDK / SaaS).
